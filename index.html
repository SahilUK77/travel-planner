Popular destination

From the magical landscapes of Europe to the amazing charm of the Bangkok, our overseas holiday deals include flights, hotels, guided sightseeing, and more – ensuring a seamless travel experience. Trending destinations like Dubai, Singapore, Thailand, Italy and Paris are perfect for families and couples alike, offering a mix of entertainment, culture, and world-class hospitality.

  • Moscow, Russia

    Moskva River

    Flowing gracefully through Moscow, the Moskva River offers a scenic backdrop to the city's iconic skyline. A river cruise reveals the contrast between modern skyscrapers and historic buildings. First impressions are of serenity amidst a bustling capital. It’s especially enchanting during sunset when the city lights reflect on the water.

  • Dubai,UAE

    Burj khalifa

    Towering above the clouds, Burj Khalifa instantly stuns visitors with its immense height and shimmering glass facade. The view from the observation deck is breathtaking, offering a panoramic view of Dubai’s desert-meets-modernity landscape. It’s a marvel of engineering and ambition standing to the height of 830 metres.

  • Tokyo,Japan

    Kyoto temple

    Set among tranquil gardens and ancient forests, Kyoto’s temples exude peace and spirituality. The wooden architecture and soft rustling of leaves make a serene first impression. It’s Kinkaku-ji’s golden reflection or Fushimi Inari’s red torii gates, each temple is a poetic escape. These architecture is a part of ancient Japanese culture.

  • Paris,France

    Eiffel Tower

    As you approach, the Eiffel Tower rises elegantly above Paris, symbolizing romance and innovation. Its intricate iron lattice design impresses both day and night. The twinkling lights at dusk leave a magical first impression, attracting admirers from all over the world. The construction work began in January,1887 and was finished on March 31,1889.

  • New Delhi,India

    Lotus Temple

    Shaped like a blooming lotus, this Baháʼí House of Worship immediately invokes calmness and unity. Surrounded by pools and gardens, it gives a sense of spiritual openness. The silence inside contrasts the noise of the city, offering peace to all visitors. The architect of the Lotus Temple was an Iranian, Fariborz Sahba.

  • Venice,Italy

    River Front

    Venice feels like stepping into a dream—gondolas gliding through canals,pastel buildings reflecting on the water. The absence of roads surprises first-timers,replaced by charming waterways and footbridges.It’s a timeless and romantic experience from the first glance.Historic architecture,the city was a major maritime power in the Middle Ages.

  • Istanbul,Turkiye

    Hagia Sophia

    Hagia Sophia, also known as Ayasofya, is a historic structure in Istanbul.It's a UNESCO World Heritage site. Standing at the crossroads of East and West, Hagia Sophia greets you with awe-inspiring domes and mosaics. Its history as a museum and mosque, adds layers to the first impression. The grandeur and sacred atmosphere are unforgettable.

  • London,UK

    Westminster

    The gothic spires of Westminster signals historical and political importance. Home to Big Ben,the iconic clock tower at the northern end of the Palace of Westminster and the UK Parliament,the area pulses with British tradition. The first impression is of solemnity, elegance & deep-rooted legacy.Completed in 1859,recognized globally for its Gothic Revival architecture and the accuracy of its clock.

  • Sydney,Australia

    Opera House

    An Opera House is a building specifically designed for opera performances, featuring a stage, orchestra pit,& seating for the audience. With its sail-like design perched on the harbor,Opera House captures attention. The contrast b/w its modern architecture & natural setting makes for a dramatic introduction. By night, it glows against the city skyline—a symbol of cultural vibrancy.

  • Singapore

    Sky-Scrapers & Nature

    It is an island country & city-state in Southeast Asia. A city of dazzling contrasts, Singapore impresses with its spotless streets, futuristic architecture & lush greenery. From the moment you land, its efficiency & innovation stand out. Gardens by the Bay & Marina Bay Sands are awe-inspiring landmarks. Singapore's unique blend of tradition & modernity, along with its cleanliness & safety, makes it popular.

  • New York,USA

    Times Square

    One of the world's busiest pedestrian intersections,it is also the hub of the Broadway Theater District & a major center of the world's entertainment industry. It overwhelms the senses with its neon lights, digital billboards & nonstop energy. The first impression is pure excitement—chaotic but captivating. It’s the heartbeat of Manhattan, alive 24/7 with people from every corner of the world.

  • Bangkok,Thailand

    Buddha Temple

    Bangkok is famous for its vibrant culture, rich history, delicious street food, bustling markets, and lively nightlife,and unique experiences like floating markets and Thai massage. Golden statues, incense-scented air, and ornate rooftops greet visitors to Thailand’s Buddha temples. The atmosphere is deeply spiritual and serene. First impressions include reverence and peace, especially in majestic sites like Wat Pho or Wat Arun.
